Who we are?

We are a company focused on revolutionizing how people invest and enhancing investors’ financial education. The main purpose is to promote the stocks market and build a community where users can connect, share, and learn. In 2010, our company was created. We started with a purpose in mind, to make the stocks market accessible to anyone. two years later, in 2012, after a lot of work, the first model of our platform was implemented in the market. Then, in 2016, our web trader was improved with new tools and features. We made an intuitive trading platform, useful for beginner and advanced traders. Finally, in 2021, we released a mobile App to facilitate the trade movements of our clients, and we decided to implement a mobile version of our platform. It can be used on Apple or Android smartphones.
